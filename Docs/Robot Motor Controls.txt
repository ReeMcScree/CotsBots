Robot Motor Controls

Motor Directions (HIGH = forward, LOW = backward)
    M1: Front right motor direction
    M2: Front left motor direction
    M3: Back right motor direction
    M4: Back left motor direction

    Can combine front and back motors and use M1 for right two and M2 for left two

Motor Speeds (0-255)
    E1: M1: Front right motor speed
    M2: Front left motor speed
    M3: Back right motor speed
    M4: Back left motor speed

    Can combine like direction

Previous Team Controls:
    Z: Print instructions
    W: Move forward indefinetly
    S: Move backward indefinetly
    A: Pivot left indefinetly
    D: Pivot right indefinetly

    W#: Move forvard for # ms (ex. w1000 for one second)
    Can be done with any direction

Future Controls:
    x,y,z
    Where x is the left motor speed, y is the right motor speed, and z is the duration of the command, in seconds (0 for continous)
